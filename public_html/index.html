About me

I am an independent editor, writer and proof reader with almost twenty years of experience in the editing and publishing industry. Since leaving Microsoft where I was an editor, I have specialized in subjects as diverse as technical, coding, medical, literary fiction, pre-publishing proofs, academic papers and speeches. I am happy to setup a Skype (or similar) conference with you to discuss your needs. I look forward to hearing from you.
